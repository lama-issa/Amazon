ICONS

The Icons collection is a permanent destination for timeless styles made with our classic bandage, designed to sculpt and mold to your shape. Each piece is crafted with eco-friendly recycled yarns, expertly knitted—not woven—and constructed in the bodycon silhouette pioneered by Hervé Léger in the ’80s. No matter the season, expect an essential offering of house signatures, from elegant column gowns to sensual interpretations of the little black dress.

About Herve Leger

Hervé Léger is an iconic luxury brand, ubiquitously known for its unforgettable bandage dresses that celebrate the female form. Under the creative direction of Christian Juul Nielsen, the brand presents collections that revisit its original Parisian roots with an undercurrent of effortless, modern design. Framed through fresh color palettes, the latest collections display the evolution of Hervé Léger which includes its signature dresses along with a new product assortment of bodysuits, skirts, tops, sweaters and jumpsuits.
